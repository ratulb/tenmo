struct CEProbabilitiesForward[dtype: DType]

Forward pass for soft probability targets.

Steps: 1. Flatten spatial: (M,C) for both logits and target 2. log_softmax + softmax along C dim 3. Apply label smoothing to target 4. loss = -sum(smoothed * log_probs, axis=1) 5. Apply reduction 6. Attach backward

Implemented traits

AnyType, Copyable, ImplicitlyCopyable, ImplicitlyDestructible, Movable, RegisterPassable
